About

I am a rising junior Computer Engineering student at the University of Notre Dame. I love making useful gadgets to accomplish my tasks! Outside of programming, I play the trombone. I am incredibly passionate about music and a member of the Notre Dame Marching Band, Hockey Band, Symphonic Winds, Band Ambassadors, and Trombone Choir.

Through my personal projects, I have played around with dozens of Raspberry Pis, Arduinos, and I also have a virtualized homelab running my servers now. I partcipate in hackathons and have learned a lot of my practical skills independently. I use linux and am incredibly familiar with Bash. I primarily program in Python and C++, but I have worked with C, JavaScript, HTML, Java, and used Verilog HDL for FPGA development.
